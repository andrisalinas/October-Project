The truth about love
Love will make you feel ways you never imagined you could
Love will not always be there to wipe your tears away
Loves eyes will make you melt in their gaze
Making you surrender everything
Love will make you feel like you finally have something to live for.
Love will change you
And Love will leave
Leave you to pick up the pieces of who you use to be.
Leave you to deal with the mess they have created.
And
No matter how many times you wish on a star
Love will not come back
