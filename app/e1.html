A PRAYING MOTHER

A PRAYING MOTHER IS A KNIGHT IN SHINING ARMOUR,
SHE NEVER SLEEPS NOR SLUMBERS,
LIKE A SHEPHERD WATCHES OVER HIS FLOCK HER EYES WILL NEVER LEAVE YOUR SIGHT,
HER KNEES ALWAYS ON THE GROUND AND YOUR NAME ALWAYS ON HER TONGUE.
A PRAYING MOTHER IS FAR MORE PRECIOUS THAN THE JEWELS OF THE WORLD,
HER LOVE PROTECTS YOU AND HER FAITH GUIDES YOU.
A PRAYING MOTHER WE SHOULD ALL BE THANKFUL FOR,
I AM THANKFUL FOR MINE..ARE YOU OF YOURS????
